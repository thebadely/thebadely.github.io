Arsenal have signed Sweden defender Smilla Holmberg on a long-term contract.

The 19-year-old joins from Swedish club Hammarby IF, having graduated from their academy to make 96 appearances.

The right-back won the league and cup double in 2023 and the cup in 2025.

Holmberg has won nine caps for Sweden and was part of the squad that reached the quarter-finals of Euro 2025.

"It's always been my dream to play for Arsenal," said Holmberg.

"This is a very special moment for me and my family.

"It's also about joining a club with the highest standards and a strong winning culture. My ambitions align with the club's and I want to help win silverware here."

Holmberg will wear the number 31 shirt and the length of her contract has not been disclosed.

"We're thrilled to welcome Smilla to the club," said manager Renee Slegers.

"She's a very talented young defender who brings versatility and athleticism, with so much potential for development."
