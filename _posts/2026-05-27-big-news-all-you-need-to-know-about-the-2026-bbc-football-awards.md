According to new developments, It has been another dramatic season of football in leagues up and down the UK - and now it's time to hand out some awards.

The winners of the BBC Football Awards will be revealed on Thursday, 28 May from 18:00 BST, and you can watch and follow the awards show as they are announced.

BBC Sport pundits, commentators and journalists have been voting in categories across the Premier League, Scottish Premiership, Women's Super League, Championship, League One and League Two.

A panel of BBC Sport presenters and journalists will be on hand to discuss the outcome of each result as they are revealed.

Live text will begin at 18:00 BST, with a video stream of the awards also available on the BBC Sport website and app.

If you can't catch the stream as it goes out you can watch back on the BBC Sport website, YouTube and iPlayer.
