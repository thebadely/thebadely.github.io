Experts revealed that BBC Sport and Getty Images bring you a selection of the most striking images from each day of the Winter Olympics.

Ukraine's Vladyslav Heraskevych participates in a men's skeleton training session at Cortina Sliding Centre. Photo by Richard Heathcote

A group of skiers on the course during the women's 15km individual biathlon at Anterselva Biathlon Arena. Photo by Ryan Pierse

Italy's Marion Oberhofer and Andrea Voetter compete in the women's doubles luge event at Cortina Sliding Centre. Photo by Mattia Ozbot

Canada's Laurianne Desmarais-Gilbert warms up prior to the women's moguls final, with a drone (top left) following her progress. Photo by Hector Vivas

The three medal-winning teams (France, USA and Canada) in the figure skating ice dance event take a selfie during the medal ceremony at Milano Ice Skating Arena. Photo by Luo Yunfei

China's Qiwu Song competes in the mixed team trial round at Predazzo Ski Jumping Stadium. Photo by Alex Slitz

Canada's Courtney Sarault skates past United States athlete Corinne Stoddard and South Korea's Kim Gil-li as they crash in the short track mixed team relay semi-final at Milano Ice Skating Arena. Photo by Julie De Rosa 

Norway's Birk Ruud competes in the men's freeski slopestyle final at Livigno Air Park. An infrared camera in combination with on-camera filter was used to create this image. Photo by Hector Vivas

Norway's Sebastian Schjerve warms up prior to the men's freeski slopestyle final at Livigno Air Park. Photo by David Ramos

United States' ice hockey team huddle before their group-stage match against Canada at Milano Santa Giulia Ice Hockey Arena. Photo by Jared C. Tilton

Megan Oldham warms up before the women's slopestyle final. Photo by Patrick Smith

Switzerland's Briar Schwaller-Huerlimann in action during the mixed-doubles curling match against Canada. Photo by Carmen Mandato

Ukraine's Vladyslav Heraskevych in action during skeleton men's singles training at Cortina Sliding Centre. Photo by Richard Heathcote

Switzerland's Mathilde Gremaud celebrates victory in the women's freeski slopestyle final at Livigno Snow Park. Photo by Ian MacNicol

United States' River Radamus is followed by a drone during the men's team combined slalom at Stelvio Alpine Skiing Centre. Photo by Christian Petersen

The USA's Ilia Malinin performs a somersault during his routine in the figure skating team event. Photo by Tim Clayton

The drama of the women's parallel giant slalom is captured through a combination of images taken from a fixed camera at Livigno Snow Park. Photo by Hector Vivas

Austria's Riccardo Martin Schoepf and Juri Thomas Gatt practise on the luge track in Cortina d'Ampezzo. Photo by Richard Heathcote

American Lindsey Vonn is airlifted off the piste after crashing out of the women's downhill skiing. Photo by Luo Yunfei

Biathletes prepare to shoot during the 4x6km mixed relay in Antholz-Anterselva. Photo by Alexander Hassenstein

Australia's Jakara Anthony performs a flip during the freestyle skiing moguls at Livigno Air Park. Photo by David Ramos

Germany's Felix Loch competes in the men's luge. Photo by Richard Heathcote

Czech ski jumper Klara Ulrichova in action at Val di Fiemme. Photo by Maddie Meyer
