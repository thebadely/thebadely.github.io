England batter Jacob Bethell will have an assessment on a finger injury, less than two weeks before the first Test against New Zealand.

The 22-year-old missed Friday's Indian Premier League game for Royal Challengers Bengaluru (RCB) against Sunrisers Hyderabad.

Defending champions RCB have qualified for the play-offs, with the final taking place on Sunday, 31 May, four days before Bethell is due to be playing for England at Lord's.

Although Bethell's absence on Friday was thought to be precautionary, the assessment is required to discover the extent of the injury.

After failing to make the RCB team at the beginning of the tournament, Bethell came into the side following an injury to fellow England international Phil Salt. In seven innings, his highest score has been 27.
