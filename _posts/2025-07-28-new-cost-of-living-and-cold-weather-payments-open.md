Here’s what you need to know: Jersey's government has opened up two grants to help islanders who are struggling financially.

The Community Costs Bonus is for people who need help with cost of living pressures, while the Cold Weather Bonus helps pensioners with heating costs.

Eligible islanders can claim £516.50 within the community costs grant, with the cold weather payments varying depending on the temperatures.

The government said people should wait until they have received their 2024 tax liability to apply.
