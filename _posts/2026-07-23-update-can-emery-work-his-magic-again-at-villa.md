So after ending their 30-year wait for a trophy with May's Europa League win, can Emery continue to produce his magic?

There was a sense at Villa Park last season they were getting into a party they were not invited to by qualifying for the Champions League. 

Villa gatecrashed the top five for the second time in three seasons, finishing fourth to comfortably secure entry to club football's elite competition for 2026-27.

Emery has achieved this after inheriting a squad that was 14th in the table, three points above the relegation zone, in November 2022.
