Position: Forward Fixture: Everton (h) Owned: 6.4%

Under Roberto de Zerbi, there has already been a marked improvement in performance levels. 

With Dominic Solanke injured, Richarlison has taken the lead striking role - notching two goals and an assist in the past four matches, while playing 90 minutes in each of the last three.

Position: Midfielder Fixture: Wolves (h)  Owned: 1.9%

The ultimate Hail Mary play. Burnley have performed well under Mike Jackson recently, with a point against Aston Villa and a narrow loss at Arsenal. 

They play their final home fixture against a Wolves team who have conceded 12 goals in their past four away games. Winger Jaidon Anthony, who already has 125 points this season, can take advantage.

Position: Midfielder Fixture: Brentford (h)  Owned: 5.6%
