According to new developments, BBC Sport and Getty Images present a selection of some of the most striking sports photographs taken around the world over the past seven days:

A diver jumps from a 22-metre-high bridge into the White Drin River during a traditional high diving competition near the town of Gjakova. Photo by Armend Nimani

Glenn Maxwell appeals for the wicket of South Africa's Ryan Rickelton during the first game of the T20  series between Australia and South Africa at Marrara Stadium in Darwin, Australia. Photo by Robert Cianflone

Blake Lawrie is tackled as his St George Illawarra Dragons face Cronulla Sharks at Netstrata Jubilee Stadium in Sydney, Australia. Photo by Darrian Traynor

Western Bulldogs' Rhylee West celebrates kicking a goal during a match against Melbourne Demons at Melbourne Cricket Ground. Photo by Morgan Hancock

Shubman Gill celebrates with team-mates Mohammed Siraj and Prasidh Krishna after India beat England in the fifth Test at the Oval to tie the series 2-2. Photo by Gareth Copley

Seattle Mariners' Dominic Canzone leaps for a foul ball during the first inning against the Tampa Bay Rays at T-Mobile Park in Seattle, Washington. Photo by Steph Chambers

Atlanta Braves' Michael Harris II reaches second base for a double as Miami Marlins' Xavier Edwards tries to apply a tag during the fourth inning at Truist Park in Atlanta, Georgia. Photo by Todd Kirkland

Chelsea's Trevoh Chalobah speaks to his team-mates before the pre-season friendly against Bayer Leverkusen at Stamford Bridge. Photo by Darren Walsh

Crystal Palace's Jean-Philippe Mateta scores from the penalty spot past Liverpool's Alisson Becker during the Community Shield at Wembley Stadium. Photo by Alex Pantling

Crystal Palace keeper Dean Henderson saves a penalty kick from Liverpool's Alexis Mac Allister in the Community Shield shootout at Wembley Stadium. Photo by Clive Mason

And Crystal Palace's Jean-Phillippe Mateta celebrates after winning the penalty shootout. Photo by Marc Atkins

Belgium's Dries De Bondt and Spain's Carlos Garcia Pierna mount a breakaway during stage one of the Vuelta a Burgos in Spain. Photo by Antonio Baixauli

The peloton pass through a tunnel during the 47th Vuelta a Burgos. Photo by Antonio Baixauli

Scottie Scheffler hits an approach to the first hole during the final round of the St. Jude Championship at TPC Southwind in Memphis, Tennessee. Photo by Stacy Revere

Sweden's Oliver Sandberg competes in the pole vault at the European Athletics U20 Championships in Tampere, Finland. Photo by Maja Hitij
