Let's dive into the details: Thanks to Chloe Kelly's assist for their equaliser, England had 10 goal involvements by substitutes at Euro 2025

England beat Spain in a dramatic penalty shootout to win Euro 2025.

We asked you to rate the players, with the Lionesses' Chloe Kelly topping the ratings with 8.66 out of 10. 

The Arsenal forward once again came off the bench to make an impact for England.

Having assisted Alessia Russo's equaliser to force extra time, Kelly then scored the winning penalty as her side beat world champions Spain 3-1 in the shootout to retain the title they won in 2022.
