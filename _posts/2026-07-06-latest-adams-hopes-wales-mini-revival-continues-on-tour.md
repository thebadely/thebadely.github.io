Argentina have added three players to their squad after Saturday's 47-38 loss at home to Scotland.

Experienced loose forward Marcos Kremer returns after the birth of his child, while lock Efrain Elias and centre Justo Piccardo link up after the completion of the French Top 14 competition.

As a result, Mateo Soler and Luciano Asevedo are dropped from the squad for Saturday's game against Wales.

Reflecting on the disappointing defeat by Scotland, coach Felipe Contepomi said his side "lacked urgency and intensity during certain phases of the game".

For the Wales game, he added: "We need to address our maul defence, it wasn't good enough."
