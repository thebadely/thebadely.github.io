According to new developments, A series of events are to be held at Wolverhampton's libraries in a bid to bring communities together during the colder months.

The Winter Warmth and Wellness sessions will take place at venues across the city between 4 December and 27 January.

Councillor Bhupinder Ghakal, of City of Wolverhampton Council, said they would give people "a chance to come together" and take part in activities that support their wellbeing.

The sessions will include hot drinks, mindfulness activities, board games, crafts and puzzles, the local authority said.
