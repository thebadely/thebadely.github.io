Experts revealed that The group stages are over and there are just eight teams left at Euro 2025, with defending champions England facing Sweden in the quarter-finals.

The BBC will broadcast three of the four last-eight ties, including the Lionesses game, and ITV will have first pick of the semi-finals.

The final on 27 July will be available on both channels.

All BBC games will also be broadcast live on iPlayer and the BBC Sport website and app, while all matches on BBC Radio will also be on BBC Sounds.

All times BST:

16 July - Norway v Italy (20:00) - BBC One

17 July - Sweden v England (20:00) - BBC One, BBC Sounds

18 July - Spain v Switzerland (20:00) - BBC One

19 July - France v Germany (20:00) - ITV
