Switzerland's Franjo von Allmen wins a third gold medal at his debut Winter Olympics, finishing fastest in the men's super-G.

Von Allmen is only the third man to have won three Olympic alpine races at a single Games.

The first was Austrian legend Toni Sailer in Cortina 70 years ago, followed by Frenchman Jean-Claude Killy in 1968.
