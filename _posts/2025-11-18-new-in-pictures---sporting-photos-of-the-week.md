Experts revealed that BBC Sport and Getty Images present the most striking sports photographs taken around the world over the past seven days.

Russia's Alisa Medvedeva competes during the Sky Grace Cup International Rhythmic Gymnastics Tournament at Beijing Shougang Ice Hockey Stadium in Beijing, China. Photo by Lintao Zhang

Northern Ireland's Rory McIlroy celebrates forcing a play-off with England's Matt Fitzpatrick on day four of the DP World Tour Championship at Jumeirah Golf Estates in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. Fitzpatrick won at the first extra hole. Photo by Andrew Redington

Conor Benn lands a punch on Chris Eubank Jr during his unanimous points victory on Saturday's 'Unfinished Business' card at Tottenham Hotspur Stadium. Photo by Justin Setterfield

England's Ben Earl and Henry Pollock celebrate at the final whistle of their 33-19 autumn international victory over New Zealand at Allianz Stadium. Photo by Warren Little

Raymond Smith in action during the ANZ Premier League at Gold Coast Convention and Exhibition Centre in Australia. Photo by Matt Roberts

Bukayo Saka celebrates scoring England's first goal during their 2-0 World Cup qualifying victory over Serbia at Wembley. Photo by Michael Regan

Melbourne Demons' Tayla Harris marks the ball during their AFLW semi-final victory over Adelaide Crows at Ikon Park in Melbourne, Australia. Photo by Morgan Hancock

Will Brown drives his Red Bull Ampol Racing Chevrolet Camaro ZL1 during practice for the Penrite Oil Sandown 500 at Sandown International Motor Raceway in Melbourne, Australia. Photo by Robert Cianflone

Sakurai flips Ohtani during an exhibition match at the Sumo + Sushi event at the Palace of Fine Arts in San Francisco, California. Photo by Ezra Shaw

Queensland's Xavier Bartlett celebrates the wicket of Western Australia's Cameron Bancroft during day four of their Sheffield Shield match in Perth, Australia. Western Australia won by one wicket. Photo by Paul Kane

Seattle Kraken's Tye Kartye battles with Winnipeg Jets' Gustav Nyquist, Logan Stanley, Colin Miller and Connor Hellebuyck in a goalmouth scramble during their NHL match at Climate Pledge Arena  in Seattle, Washington. Seattle won the match 5-3. Photo by Steph Chambers

Malcolm Wellmaker strikes Ethyn Ewing during UFC 322 at Madison Square Garden in New York City. Ewing caused an upset as he won by unanimous decision. Photo by Ishika Samant

Texas A&M Aggies celebrate their 31-30 win over the South Carolina Gamecocks at Kyle Field in College Station, Texas. Photo by Alex Slitz

Italy's Jannik Sinner celebrates beating Spain's Carlos Alcaraz at the ATP Finals at Inalpi Arena in Turin, Italy. Photo by Daniel Kopatsch

Sydney's Kirsty Fenton tackles Rola Badawiya during her team's 2-0 victory over Perth Glory in the A-League at Leichhardt Oval in Sydney, Australia. Photo by Cameron Spencer

Illawarra Hawks' Mason Peatling drives to the basket during their NBL defeat by Sydney Kings at WIN Entertainment Centre, in Wollongong, Australia. Photo by Darrian Traynor
