Let's dive into the details: BBC Sport and Getty Images present a selection of some of the most striking sports photographs taken around the world over the past seven days:

Great Britain's Lando Norris driving his McLaren during practice before the Dutch Grand Prix at Zandvoort. Photo by Alastair Staley

Fans hold up coloured sheets to form the Netherlands flag in a grandstand prior to the Dutch GP. Photo by Clive Rose

Spain's Carlos Alcaraz serves to France's Arthur Rinderknech during the fourth round of the US Open at Flushing Meadows. Photo by Clive Brunskill

Everton's Jack Grealish celebrates with fans after his team's Premier League win against Wolves at Molineux. Photo by Dan Istitene

Manchester United's Bruno Fernandes celebrates with Diogo Dalot after scoring his side's winner in a 3-2 victory against Burnley at Old Trafford. Photo by Daniel Chesterton

Fans of San Lorenzo prior to a Torneo Clausura Betano 2025 match against Huracan at Pedro Bidegain Stadium in Buenos Aires, Argentina. Photo by Marcelo Endelli

United States' Elizabeth Szokol loses her sunglasses as she drives off the 15th tee during the second round of the LPGA Tour's FM Championship in Massachusetts. Photo by Michael Owens

Kansas Jayhawks' Daniel Hishaw Jr is seized by Sam Martin of the Wagner Seahawks in their Big 12 match at David Booth Kansas Memorial Stadium. Photo by Jay Biggerstaff

Sunset during the NASCAR Cup Series Southern 500 at Darlington Raceway in South Carolina. Photo by Jared C. Tilton

Australia's Troy Brosnan competes during the UCI Mountain Bike World Series in Les Gets, France. Photo by Billy Ceusters

St Kilda Saints' Jesse Wardlaw celebrates kicking a goal during an AFLW match against West Coast Eagles at RSEA Park in Melbourne, Australia. Photo by Graham Denholm

Runners pass under Harbour Bridge during the 2025 Sydney Marathon. Photo by Darrian Traynor

Southland celebrate after winning the Ranfurly Shield against Waikato in Hamilton, New Zealand. Photo by Hannah Peters

Team Visma ride in formation during the fifth stage - a team time trial in Figueres - of La Vuelta in Spain. Photo by Tim de Waele

Welsh Fires' Ben Kellaway waits to bat during The Hundred match between Southern Brave and Welsh Fire at Utilita Bowl. Photo by Alex Davidson

Trent Rockets' Ben Cox runs out Oval Invincibles' Tom Curran during the final of The Hundred at Lord's. Photo by Philip Brown

Competitors finish the swimming leg of the 2025 Ironman 70.3 in Zell am See, Austria. Photo by Jan Hetfleisch
