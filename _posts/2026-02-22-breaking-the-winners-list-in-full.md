Here’s what you need to know: The winners of the Bafta Film Awards, which celebrate the best on the big screen from the past 12 months, have been revealed.

One Battle After Another won the most awards with six, followed by I Swear, Sinners and Frankenstein, which got three each, while Hamnet won two.
