According to new developments, BBC Sport and Getty Images present a selection of some of the most striking sports photographs taken around the world over the past seven days...

Australia's Reece Walsh goes airborne to score the opening try during their Rugby League Ashes victory over England at Wembley. Photo by Michael Steele

Seattle Seahawks' Jaxon Smith-Njigba walks through a laser tunnel before their NFL match against the Houston Texans at Lumen Field in Seattle, Washington. Photo by Steph Chambers

Race winner Lando Norris celebrates at the end of the Mexico City Grand Prix. Photo by Bryn Lennon

Real Madrid's Kylian Mbappe scores a goal - later disallowed after a VAR review - during their El Clasico victory over Barcelona in Madrid. Photo by Angel Martinez

Ryan Blaney waits backstage during the pre-race ceremonies before the Nascar Cup Series Xfinity 500 at Martinsville Speedway in Virginia. Photo by Jared C. Tilton

The Magpies' Jordyn Allen and the Suns' Jacqueline Dupuy compete for the ball during the AFLW match between their sides. Photo by Chris Hyde

Red Bull Ampol Racing's Broc Feeney takes to the air during the Gold Coast 500 - part of the 2025 Supercars Championship in Surfers Paradise, Australia. Photo by Daniel Kalisz

Ella Slocombe is congratulated by team-mates after making her AFLW debut for the North Melbourne Kangaroos against St Kilda Saints in Hobart, Australia. Photo by Steve Bell

Bruno Guimaraes celebrates after scoring Newcastle's second goal in their Premier League victory over Fulham at St James' Park. Photo by Serena Taylor

The Amarris Class 40 monohull - skippered by Achille Nebout and Gildas Mahe - starts the 17th edition of the Transat Cafe L'Or - off the shores of Le Havre. Photo by Loic Venance

Ferrari's Charles Leclerc competes during practice for the Mexico City Grand Prix. Photo by Bryn Lennon

Matt Wallace drives at the fourth tee during the first round of the Bank of Utah Championship at the Black Desert Resort in St George. Photo by Justin Edmonds

Texas Tech Red Raiders' Cameron Dickey runs for a touchdown during the first half of their match against the Oklahoma State Cowboys in Lubbock, Texas. Photo by John E. Moore III

An aerial view of the Rogers Centre as Pharrell performs the Canadian national anthem prior to Game One of the World Series between the Los Angeles Dodgers and the Toronto Blue Jays. Photo by Daniel Shirey

Queretaro's Rodrigo Bogarin fights for the ball with Chivas' Roberto Alvarado during a league match at La Corregidora Stadium in Mexico. Photo by Rodrigo Oropeza

Boston Celtics' Neemias Queta dunks the ball in the fourth quarter of their NBA match against the Philadelphia 76ers in Boston, Massachusetts. Photo by Adam Glanzman

Japan celebrate Honoka Sunaga's goal during their Under-17 Women's World Cup match against Zambia in Rabat, Morocco. Photo by Joern Pollex

Arsenal's Viktor Gyokeres celebrates after scoring their fourth goal during the Champions League victory over Atletico Madrid. Photo by James Gill
