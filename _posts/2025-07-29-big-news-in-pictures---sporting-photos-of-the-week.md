According to new developments, BBC Sport and Getty Images present a selection of some of the most striking sports photographs taken around the world over the past seven days:

England's Leah Williamson, Georgia Stanway, Esme Morgan and Keira Walsh celebrate alongside the Euro 2025 trophy as they lay in ticker tape after victory over Spain. Photo by Harriet Lander

England's Hannah Hampton saves a penalty kick from Spain's Mariona Caldentey during the Euro 2025 final. Photo by Daniela Porcelli

England's Beth Mead and Ella Toone celebrate following their side's victory over Spain. Photo by Maja Hitij

England's Michelle Agyemang is congratulated by Prince William after the Euro 2025 final. Photo by Harry Langer

A double rainbow is seen over Fenway Park during the first inning of the game between the Boston Red Sox and the Los Angeles Dodgers in Boston, Massachusetts. Photo By Winslow Townson

Washington Nationals' Jacob Young catches a ball hit by Cincinnati Reds' Will Benson during the eighth inning at Nationals Park in Washington, DC. Photo by Jess Rapfogel

Seattle Storm's Nneka Ogwumike gathers her team-mates before their WNBA game against the Dallas Wings at Climate Pledge Arena in Seattle, Washington. Photo by Steph Chambers

Dallas Wings' Paige Bueckers looks on as Seattle Storm's Gabby Williams and team-mate Li Yueru battle for the rebound at Climate Pledge Arena in Seattle, Washington. Photo by Steph Chambers

Reece Walsh of the Brisbane Broncos celebrates scoring a last-minute try against the Paramatta Eels at Suncorp Stadium, only for it to be disallowed for offside. Photo by Chris Hyde

United States compete in the Team Technical Final at the Singapore 2025 World Aquatics Championships in Singapore. Photo by Adam Pretty

Luke Littler celebrates with The Phil Taylor Trophy after victory against James Wade at the World Matchplay at Winter Gardens in Blackpool. Photo by Lewis Storey

Tadej Pogacar moves ahead of rival Jonas Vingegaard while racing up Mount Ventoux during the Tour de France. Photo by Tim de Waele

Japan's Yuki Tsunoda during qualifying for the Belgium Grand Prix at Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps. Photo by Alex Bierens de Haan

Germany's Melvin Imoudu competes in the 100m breaststroke at the World Aquatics Championships in Singapore. Photo by Quinn Rooney
