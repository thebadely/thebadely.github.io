It has been recently reported that BBC Sport and Getty Images present a selection of some of the most striking sports photographs taken around the world over the past seven days:

Carlos Sainz of Williams prepares for a practice session before the British Grand Prix at Silverstone. Photo by Mark Sutton

(L-R) Kaio Cesar, Jhon Arias and Sergej Milinkovic-Savic battle for possession during the Club World Cup quarter-final between Fluminense and Al-Hilal in Florida. Photo by Alex Grimm

Dalma Galfi prepares to hit a forehand against Harriet Dart during the ladies' singles first round on day one of Wimbledon. Photo by Hannah Peters

Facundo Bernal celebrates German Cano scoring Fluminense's first goal in their win against Inter Milan in the Club World Cup in Charlotte, North Carolina. Photo by Jared C. Tilton

Players and match officials pause for a moment of silence before the Bayern Munich v Paris St-Germain Club World Cup match in memory of former Liverpool player Diogo Jota and his brother Andre Silva. Photo by Patrick Smith

A spectator waves a flare as Jayco AlUla rider Mauro Schmid rides an ascent in the final kilometres of the second stage of the Tour de France. Photo by Anne-Christine Poujoulat

Carlos Alcaraz plays a backhand volley against Oliver Tarvet during the second round of the men's singles at Wimbledon. Photo by Clive Brunskill

Lautaro Martinez of Inter Milan looks on from the tunnel prior to the Club World Cup match against Fluminense. Photo by Steph Chambers

Cristiana Girelli and Emma Severini of Italy battle for possession with Marie Detruyer and Sarah Wijnants of Belgium during the Women's Euro 2025 match in Sion, Switzerland. Photo by Eddie Keogh

Walker Buehler of the Boston Red Sox pitches against the Washington Nationals during the sixth inning in Washington, DC. Photo by Scott Taetsch

Fran Garcia of Real Madrid celebrates scoring his team's second goal during the Club World Cup quarter-final against Borussia Dortmund. Photo by Dustin Satloff

Shane van Gisbergen celebrates with a burnout after winning the NASCAR Xfinity Series in Chicago. Photo by Chris Graythen

Isabelle Kelly is tackled from behind during a NRLW match between the Sydney Roosters and zthe New Zealand Warriors in Sydney, Australia. Photo by Jeremy Ng

Gonzalo Garcia of Real Madrid scores his team's first goal past Gregor Kobel of Borussia Dortmund during the Club World Cup quarter-final in New Jersey. Photo by Luke Hales

Isaiah Dudley of the Fremantle Dockers celebrates kicking a goal against the Sydney Swans during round 17 of the AFL season. Photo by Mark Metcalfe

Oliver Bearman drives his Haas through wet conditions during the British Grand Prix at Silverstone. Photo by Clive Mason

Chad Warner of the Swans celebrates beating the Fremantle Dockers in Sydney. Photo by Darrian Traynor

Shubman Gill of India speaks to his team-mates in a huddle during the final day of the second Test against England at Edgbaston. Photo by Alex Davidson

The Minnesota Twins celebrate after Harrison Bader hit a walk-off home run in the ninth inning of a game against the Tampa Bay Rays in Minneapolis. Photo by Ellen Schmidt

The All Blacks perform the haka before the Test against France in Dunedin, New Zealand. Photo by Phil Walter
