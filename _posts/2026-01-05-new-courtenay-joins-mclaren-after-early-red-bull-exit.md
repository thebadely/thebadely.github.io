Former Red Bull head of strategy Will Courtenay has started his role as McLaren's sporting director as Formula 1 returns to work after its winter break.

Courtenay officially joined McLaren on 2 January 2026, a spokesman for the team confirmed without further comment.

Courtenay had signed to join McLaren in September 2024 but was initially being held to his contract, which ran some months into 2026.

However, the advent of new leadership at Red Bull following the sacking of Christian Horner in July led to talks over Courtenay's future reopening, and a compromise has been reached between the two teams.
