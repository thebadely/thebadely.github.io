US Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) agents have been told to immediately halt most vehicle stops nationwide, following two fatal shootings involving ICE.

The latest happened in Maine when an ICE agent shot and killed a 26-year-old Colombian man in the city of Biddeford. 

The Department of Homeland Security said the officer opened fire after "fearing for public safety".
