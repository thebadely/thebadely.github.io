According to new developments, BBC Sport and Getty Images present a selection of some of the most striking sports photographs taken around the world over the past seven days:

Denny Hamlin of Toyota and Kyle Larson of Chevrolet lead the field on a pace lap prior to the NASCAR Cup Series race in Madison, Illinois. Photo by Chris Graythen

Ernest John Obiena of the Philippines competes in the men's pole vault during the World Athletics Tour meeting in Beijing, China. Photo by Lintao Zhang

Lewis Hamilton of Ferrari walks in the paddock prior to the Italian Grand Prix at Monza. Photo by Kym Illman

A fan wears a 'Make Ferrari Great Again' cap before the Italian Grand Prix. Photo by Clive Rose

Carlos Sainz of Williams during final practice before the Italian Grand Prix. Photo by Peter Fox

Winner Max Verstappen of Red Bull plus runner-up Lando Norris and third-placed Oscar Piastri, both of McLaren, celebrate on the podium at Monza. Photo by Mario Renzi

Alex Marquez holds off Marc Marquez on his way to victory in the MotoGP Grand Prix in Barcelona. Photo by David Ramos

Jacob Bethell celebrates his century during the one-day international between England and South Africa at Utilita Bowl, Southampton. Photo by Harry Trump

The peloton on stage six of the 27th Simac Ladies Tour in Lichtenvoorde, the Netherlands. Photo by Luc Claessen

Maika Hamano celebrates scoring Chelsea's second goal during the Women's Super League match against Manchester City at Stamford Bridge. Photo by Justin Setterfield

Northern Ireland's Rory McIlroy plays his second shot on the 17th hole on day one of the Amgen Irish Open at The K Club in Straffan, Republic of Ireland. Photo by Richard Heathcote

Carlos Alcaraz celebrates after his four-set victory against Jannik Sinner of Italy in the men's singles final of the US Open in New York. Photo by Clive Brunskill

Aryna Sabalenka poses with her trophy after defeating Amanda Anisimova of the United States in the US Open women's singles. Photo by Elsa

Japan's Tokito Oda celebrates after winning the US Open wheelchair men's singles final against Gustavo Fernandez of Argentina. Photo by Clive Brunskill

Brisbane Lions' Ty Gallop and Josh Dunkley compete against Geelong Cats' Lawson Humphries for a mark during the AFL qualifying final at the MCG in Australia. Photo by Daniel Pockett

Chicago Sky's Kamilla Cardoso is fouled by Indiana Fever's Damiris Dantas during the third quarter of their WNBA game at Gainbridge Fieldhouse in Indianapolis. Photo by Justin Casterline

The Louisville Cardinals' Isaac Brown runs the ball against the James Madison Dukes' cornerback Elijah Culp in Louisville, Kentucky. Photo by Michael Hickey

Cristiano Ronaldo celebrates scoring Portugal's fourth goal in their World Cup qualifier against Armenia in Yerevan. Photo by Denis Tyrin
