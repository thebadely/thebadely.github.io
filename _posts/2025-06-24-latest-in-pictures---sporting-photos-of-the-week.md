Let's dive into the details: BBC Sport and Getty Images present a selection of some of the most striking sports photographs taken around the world over the past seven days:

Monterrey's Nelson Deossa is fouled by River Plate's Maximiliano Meza during a Club World Cup match at the Rose Bowl Stadium in California. Photo by Stu Forster

America's Denny McCarthy plays a shot on the sixth hole at the Travelers Championship in Cromwell, Connecticut. Photo by Andrew Redington

India's Rishabh Pant celebrates reaching his century during the first Test against England at Headingley. Photo by Clive Mason

Fans hold scarves during the Club World Cup match between Inter Milan and Urawa Red Diamonds in Seattle. Photo by Justin Setterfield

Mackenzie McDonald serves against Gael Monfils during their first-round match at Queen's. Photo by Julian Finney

Christian Lio-Willie celebrates with his Crusaders team-mates after beating the Chiefs to win the Super Rugby Pacific trophy in Christchurch. Photo by Joe Allison

Flamengo's Luiz Araujo takes a corner during their Club World Cup game against Chelsea in Philadelphia. Photo by Dustin Satloff

Ben King marks the ball during the AFL match between Greater Western Sydney Giants and Gold Coast Suns in Sydney. Photo by Matt King

Karim Onisiwo scores Red Bull Salzburg's second goal during their Club World Cup match against Pachuca in Cincinnati. Photo by Michael Regan

Francisco Rodriguez Jr catches Galal Yafai as they fight for the interim WBC super-flyweight title at BP Pulse Live in Birmingham. Photo by Mark Robinson

North Melbourne Kangaroos'  Tristan Xerri competes with Carlton Blues' Tom de Koning during their AFL match at the Melbourne Cricket Ground. Photo by Robert Cianflone

Boca Juniors' Rodrigo Battaglia celebrates after scoring his team's second goal during the Club World Cup match against Benfica in Florida. Photo by Dan Mullan

Jalen Williams of the Oklahoma City Thunder celebrates a dunk against the Indiana Pacers during the 2025 NBA Finals in Oklahoma City. Photo by Matthew Stockman

Inter Miami's Lionel Messi walks out of the tunnel prior to their Club World Cup match against Porto in Atlanta. Photo by Patrick Smith

The Florida Panthers celebrate winning the Stanley Cup with victory against the Edmonton Oilers in Florida. Photo by Bruce Bennett

Boston Red Sox's Abraham Toro is out at home plate after attempting to leap over San Francisco Giants' catcher Patrick Bailey in an MLB match. Photo by Thearon W. Henderson
