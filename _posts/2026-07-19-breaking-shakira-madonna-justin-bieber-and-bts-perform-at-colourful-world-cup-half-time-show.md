Stars shine at half-time show

Pop giants Madonna, BTS, Shakira and Justin Bieber have performed for thousands of spectators at the World Cup final in a half-time show to rival the Superbowl.

Madonna, who sung her hit song Music - seemed to appear from tunnels under the arena, joined by Brazilian football legends Ronaldo and Ronaldinho.

The performances came in a half-time that ran to 27 minutes and 22 seconds - the longest in World Cup history.
