It has been recently reported that BBC Sport and Getty Images present a selection of some of the most striking sports photographs taken around the world over the past seven days:

United States' Scottie Scheffler celebrates with his son Bennett Scheffler and the Claret Jug on the 18th green after winning The 153rd Open Championship at Royal Portrush Golf Club in Northern Ireland. Photo by Christian Petersen

Dunluce Castle is seen as Rory McIlroy of Northern Ireland putts on the fifth green during Day Three of The 153rd Open Championship at Royal Portrush Golf Club. Photo by Christian Petersen

A general view of racing during the NASCAR Xfinity Series BetRivers 200 at Dover Motor Speedway on July 19, 2025 in Dover, Delaware. Photo by Sean Gardner

Dustin Poirier strikes Max Holloway during their lightweight bout during UFC 318. Photo by Jonathan Bachman

British and Irish Lions' Tommy Freeman gets past Australia's Joseph-Aukuso Suaalii during the 1st Test Match between Australia Wallabies and British & Irish Lions in Brisbane, Australia. Photo by David Rogers

British and Irish Lions' Sione Tuipulotu celebrates after scoring a try during game one of the series between Australia Wallabies and British & Irish Lions at Suncorp Stadium in Brisbane, Australia. Photo by Chris Hyde

England's Ben Stokes appeals succesfully for the wicket of KL Rahul of India during Day Five of the 3rd Rothesay Test Match between England and India at Lord's Cricket Ground. Photo by Clive Mason

Sweden's Stina Blackstenius battles for possession with England's Esme Morgan during the UEFA Women's EURO 2025 Quarter-Final match in Zurich, Switzerland. Photo by Alex Caparros

Germany's Ann-Katrin Berger celebrates victory with teammate Linda Dallmann after defeating France 6-5 in the penalty shootout during the UEFA Women's EURO 2025 Quarter-Final in Basel, Switzerland. Photo by Maja Hitij

Team Italy's Agnese Cocchiere is fouled in the Women's Water Polo crossover match between Italy and China on day seven of the Singapore 2025 World Aquatics Championships. Photo by Quinn Rooney

Team Japan compete in the Open's Team Free Preliminaries on day nine of the Singapore 2025 World Aquatics Championships. Photo by Adam Pretty

Japan's Tomoka Miyazaki captured in her Badminton match in the Women's Singles First Round against Scotland's Kirsty Gilmour in Tokyo, Japan. Photo by Shi Tang

United States' Matthew Marquardt competes in the bike leg as part of the 2025 Athletic Brewing IRONMAN in Lake Placid, New York. Photo by Hector Vivas
