Goals from Zack Wallace and Samuel Hooper put England in a strong position after 10 minutes, but Tijmen Reyenga reduced the deficit shortly before half-time and Miles Bukkens equalised with just three minutes remaining to force a shootout.

England return to action on Saturday against Spain at 10:00 GMT in Valencia.
