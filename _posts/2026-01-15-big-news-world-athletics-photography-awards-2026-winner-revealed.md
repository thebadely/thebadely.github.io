It has been recently reported that BBC Sport and the World Sport Photography Awards present a selection of the world's finest sporting images from this year's competition. Displayed below are Gold, Silver, Bronze and Special Merit winners across all 24 categories of the awards, now in their sixth year.

Carlos' Shadow Hits A Ball by Edgar Su

Spain's Carlos Alcaraz in action during his fourth-round match against Britain's Jack Draper at the 2025 Australian Open

The Underworld by Beatrice Ryder da Costa

Surfer rising from under the wave in the Solomon Islands

India v England layers by Richard Heathcote

Layered view from the Lord's Nursery End, with India in control of the opening morning's play of the third Test against England.

American football: One Player. 110,000 Screaming Fans. by Christian Gresko

Oregon Ducks tight end Roger Saleapaga looks towards the famous and animated Penn State University "White Out"

Athletics: Tokyo Rocket by Kenjiro Matsuo

Japanese javelin thrower Momone Ueda launches a "rocket" into the Tokyo sky during the 2025 World Athletics Championships

Baseball: Bubble by Steph Chambers

Eugenio Suarez of the Seattle Mariners blows bubble gum during batting practice prior to game five of the American League Division Series against the Detroit Tigers

Basketball: Breakaway for the Lead by Bob Donnan

Florida guard Alijah Martin gives the Gators the lead for good on a breakaway score in the semi-finals of the NCAA tournament

Boxing: Uppercut by Alexis Goudeau

Tony Yoka receives an uppercut punch from Arslan Yellyav during their fight in Paris

Cycling: Vistas desde el abandono by Aritz Arambarri Goenaga

A parade of cyclists seen through panes of a partially broken window in a nearby abandoned building during the Copa de Espana Torredonjimeno

Equestrian: Equine Reflection by Morgan Treacy

Runners and riders reflected in the sand on the beach in Laytown, Ireland's only horse racing event run on a beach under the Rules of Racing

Football: Black & White Passion by Luis Amaral

A father and child wildly celebrate an Atletico Mineiro goal

Formula 1: Tunnel Exit by Luca Martini

A Red Bull car, captured through the Armco barriers, zooms through the tunnel during the Monaco Grand Prix

Golf: Tommy Fleetwood emerges from a hedge by David Cannon

Tommy Fleetwood steps out from the bushes during the Dubai Desert Classic

Gymnastics: Rhythmic Gymnastics World Championships - Rio de Janeiro 2025 by Buda Mendes

Meital Maayan Sumkin performing with a ball and balancing on one hand at the Rhythmic Gymnastics World Championships 2025 in Rio de Janeiro

Ice hockey: Last Line of Defence by Sophia Sandurskaya

The Megasport Stadium in Moscow plays host to the Kontinental Hockey League match between Spartak Moscow and the Shanghai Dragons

Martial arts: Unusual Sumo Attack by Tom Jenkins

Tobizaru sticks a finger up the nose of Takayasu as they fight during the final day of the five-day Grand Sumo Tournament at the Royal Albert Hall in London

Motor Sports: Emerging Ford by Christian Gresko

Rally car eating dust at Shakedown during the 2025 WRC Safari Rally Kenya

Other: Bout by Anna Zinovieva

Fencing bout exposing the bright neon lights in Russia

Racquet Sports: Kim Nayeong - WTT Foz do Iguacu by Abelardo Mendes Jr

Kim Nayeong of South Korea with her eye on the ball mid-serve at the WTT Star Contender Foz do Iguacu tournament

Rugby union: New Zealand Haka by Andrew Dowling

The All Blacks perform the Haka at Principality Stadium in Cardiff

Swimming & Diving: Catching a Breath by Carel du Plessis

Henry Bramwell-Reeks, a triathlete from the United Kingdom, during the swim leg of a full-distance triathlon

Urban & Extreme: Klickflippin' India by Martin Bissig

An skateboarder finds an improbable line, launching across the void of the ancient Jaipur stepwell

Venues & Views: Championship View by Andrew Hancock

Confetti falls from the circular video board atop the Mercedes-Benz Stadium as the Ohio State Buckeyes rush the field to celebrate winning the 2025 College Football Playoff National Championship

Winter Sports: North by Germain Favre-Felix

Skier Richard Permin surfing the wave of snow, leaving a fleeting trace in the endless, snowy canvas
