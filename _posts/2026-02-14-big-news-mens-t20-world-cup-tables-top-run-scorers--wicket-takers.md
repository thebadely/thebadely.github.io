Experts revealed that USA's Shadley van Schalkwyk (L) and New Zealand's Tim Seifert (R) are the current leading wicket-takers and run-scorers at the Men's T20 World Cup

The T20 World Cup group stage is well under way in India and Sri Lanka.

But which players are topping the run-scoring and wicket-taking charts?

BBC Sport digs out all of the latest numbers.

*Top two in each group progress to the Super 8s, which will be made up of two groups of four.

If teams are level on points they are separated by number of wins and the net run-rate.

After the Super 8s fixtures, the top two teams progress to the semi-finals. The winner of each Super 8s group faces the runner-up in the other.
