It has been recently reported that Founded by Omani sisters, Elyamm is a newly launched fine jewellery brand redefining timeless elegance through a modern lens. Rooted in storytelling and craftsmanship, each creation reflects a balance of heritage and contemporary sophistication, pieces designed to move seamlessly from day to night and be treasured for generations. Its debut collection, Zigzag, reimagines the iconic motif found across art, nature, and architecture into sculptural 18k gold and diamond pieces that mirror life’s rhythm, its highs, lows, and moments of harmony. With a design philosophy anchored in emotion, versatility, and meaning, Elyamm celebrates individuality through jewellery that feels both personal and poetic, a refreshing addition to Oman’s growing creative landscape and a brand to watch on the fine jewellery scene.
