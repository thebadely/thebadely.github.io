Here’s what you need to know: Nestled in Alserkal Avenue, Middle Child by Chef Lynn Hazim redefines comfort dining with a playful, creative twist. Blending a cozy café, cookbook haven, and gourmet grocery, this minimalist space invites diners to gather around its communal table for elevated comfort dishes that feel both nostalgic and new. Born in Beirut and now based in Dubai, Hazim channels her No Soup For You charm into a space where food, culture, and curiosity come together. making Middle Child the city’s newest hub for delicious bites and inspired conversations. Located in Alserkal Avenue, it’s open from Wednesday to Sunday, 12:30pm to 10pm.
