It has been recently reported that BBC Sport and Getty Images present the most striking sports photographs taken around the world over the past seven days.

Real Madrid's Kylian Mbappe is awarded the European Golden Boot as the top scorer in Europe's leagues for 2024-25 at Estadio Santiago Bernabeu in Madrid, Spain. Photo by Antonio Villalba.

India's Harmanpreet Kaur celebrates with the ICC Women's Cricket World Cup trophy as team-mates wave the national flag of India following victory over South Africa in Navi Mumbai, India. Photo by Alex Davidson.

Los Angeles Dodgers' Yoshinobu Yamamoto celebrates defeating the Toronto Blue Jays 5-4 in game seven of the 2025 World Series at Rogers Center in Toronto, Ontario. Photo by Gregory Shamus.

Melbourne Stars' Jasmine Kamboj bowls during the T20 Spring Challenge match against Perth Scorchers at Blacktown International Sports Park in Sydney, Australia. Photo by Mark Metcalfe.

Mark Zahra rides Observer to victory in race seven at the Howden Victoria Derby at Flemington Racecourse in Melbourne, Australia. Photo by Robert Cianflone.

The Philadelphia Flyers' Noah Juulsen body checks the Pittsburgh Penguins' Tommy Novak at Xfinity Mobile Arena in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. The Flyers defeated the Penguins 3-2 in the shootout. Photo by Bruce Bennett.

The Los Angeles Dodgers' Freddie Freeman is tagged out at home by the Toronto Blue Jays' Alejandro Kirk during the third inning in game three of the 2025 World Series at Dodger Stadium in Los Angeles, California. Photo by Harry How.

England's Henry Pollock celebrates scoring his team's second try with team-mate Luke Cowan-Dickie during the Quilter Nations Series 2025 rugby international match between England and Australia at Allianz Stadium in London. Photo by Shaun Botterill.

France's Jean-Eric Vergne driving the Citroen Racing e-CX smokes his tyres as he exits the pit lane during Formula E pre-Season testing at Ricardo Tormo Circuit in Valencia, Spain. Photo by Simon Galloway.

Erling Haaland scores Manchester City's second goal past Bournemouth's Djordje Petrovic at Etihad Stadium. Photo by James Gill.

India's Amanjot Kaur celebrates with team-mate Jemimah Rodrigues after hitting a four to win the ICC Women's Cricket World Cup India 2025 semi-final match at DY Patil Stadium in Navi Mumbai, India. Photo by Pankaj Nangia.

Tonga's Daniel Tupou scores a try during the Rugby League Pacific Championships match against the New Zealand Kiwis at Eden Park in Auckland, New Zealand. Photo by Hannah Peters.

West Ham's Tomas Soucek scores his team's third goal at London Stadium. Photo by Justin Setterfield.

The Power's Ebony O'Dea is challenged by the Giants' Tarni Evans during the AFLW Round 12 match between Greater Western Sydney Giants and Port Adelaide Power at Henson Park in Sydney, Australia. Photo by Matt King.

The Toronto Blue Jays' George Springer sits on the home plate during the 10th inning against the Los Angeles Dodgers in game seven of the 2025 World Series at Rogers Center in Toronto, Ontario. Photo by Patrick Smith.

Bertrand Rhodict Anak Lises competes in the men's 10m platform preliminaries on day three of the Malaysia Open Diving Championships 2025 at the National Aquatics Centre in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. Photo by Pakawich Damrongkiattisak.
