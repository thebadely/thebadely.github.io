Let's dive into the details: Celebrate New Year’s Eve at new heights with the return of SAMBAZONIA, SUSHISAMBA’s iconic all-white celebration in the sky. Drawing inspiration from the mystique of the Amazon rainforest and Brazil’s vibrant Réveillon tradition, the night unfolds with electrifying live entertainment, elevated culinary creations and signature cocktails. As midnight strikes, guests are treated to panoramic views of Dubai’s fireworks, making it a high-energy and unforgettable way to welcome 2026.

The SAMBAZONIA White Party experience is priced at Dhs2,900 per person for regular tables, Dhs3,900 per person for window tables, and Dhs2,300 per person for round booths (minimum of five guests).

For more information visit sushisamba.com

Located at St. Regis Gardens, this contemporary Korean dining destination ushers in the New Year with an elevated evening of fine gastronomy. Led by Chef Kyung Soo Moon, the New Year’s Eve celebration features an enhanced à la carte menu showcasing a modern interpretation of Korean flavours, with a minimum spend of Dhs1,200 per person.

For more information visit hanu-restaurant.com

Chalet Berezka is a standout choice for a high-energy New Year’s Eve in Dubai. Expect a chic crowd, a striking terrace, immersive lighting, and live performances that keep the celebration going well past midnight.

The New Year’s Eve menu leans indulgent with highlights including premium caviar with classic garnishes, the original Olivier salad with beef tongue, chicken, and shrimp, and orange-cured salmon. Mains range from a grilled seafood platter to Australian Wagyu beef tenderloin with triple-cooked potatoes and pepper sauce. Medovik, the classic Russian honey cake, finishes the night.

Price starts from Dhs575. For more information, visit berezka.ae/events and for reservations, call +971 52 156 7405

This New Year’s Eve, XU rolls out the red carpet and invites guests to welcome 2026 in true movie-star style with its Brunchflix special edition. Designed as a cinematic celebration, the evening blends dazzling live entertainment with high-energy DJ sets, festive live cooking stations and interactive karaoke that lets guests take centre stage.

The celebration runs from 8pm until late on December 31, with a range of packages to suit every style of celebration, including a non-alcoholic package at Dhs325, house beverages at Dhs450, Prosecco at Dhs490, and a premium beverage package at Dhs625.

For more information visit xudubai.ae

This festive season, Gohan brings its signature Tokyo-meets-Dubai energy to the forefront with a line-up of celebrations culminating in an electric New Year’s Eve beneath the glow of Ain Dubai. On December 31 (8pm–12am), the venue transforms into a full-scale beachside party as guests enjoy a curated set menu priced at Dhs1,200 per person with beverages available à la carte. As midnight approaches, the terrace buzzes with music, raised glasses and rising anticipation, while fireworks light up the shoreline just steps away.

For more information visit gohanbluewaters.ae

Kickstart 2026 in style at Nammos Dubai, where the iconic beachfront hotspot promises an unforgettable New Year’s Day. Guests will be treated to an exclusive performance by PAWSA, spinning his signature tech-house beats amidst the glamour of the Mediterranean-inspired setting. Exclusive packages start from Dhs10,000 for up to 10 guests, making it the perfect way to welcome the new year with friends in ultimate luxury.

For more information visit nammos.com

RASPOUTINE Dubai, one of the iconic hotspot is serving up a sensory feast of French hospitality, exquisite cuisine, and signature deep house beats. From 9th to 27th December, guests can indulge in a meticulously crafted Christmas menu featuring highlights like Beluga Caviar with Walnut Ice Cream. As the year winds down, RASPOUTINE transforms into a dazzling New Year’s Eve Gala, with live performances, DJ sets from Dubai and Paris, and a glamorous celebration for a minimum spend of Dhs1,000 per person. Dress to impress and immerse yourself in an unforgettable evening of festive elegance and Parisian flair.

For more information visit raspoutine.com

Set high above the Marina at Ciel Dubai, West 13 offers a glamorous New Year’s Eve gala from 7pm to midnight. Packages are priced at Dhs850 including house beverages, or Dhs1,200 with premium and sparkling beverages, plus access to the after-party.

For those craving bold flavours, East 14 at Ciel Dubai Marina presents a vibrant culinary journey inspired by India, Thai street favourites, and Southeast Asian creations. Taking place from 7pm to 12am, the evening blends aromatic dishes with dynamic live performances for a truly immersive celebration. Pricing starts at Dhs850 with house beverages, while Dhs1,200 includes premium and sparkling beverages along with after-party access.

Keep the celebrations going under the stars at Nuage, Ciel Dubai Marina’s stunning pool deck on the 11th floor. From 11pm to 1am, guests are invited to dance into 2026 with DJ-led beats, free-flowing drinks, and sweeping views of the Marina skyline. Entry is priced at Dhs350 per person, making it the perfect late-night finale to New Year’s Eve.

For more information visit cieldubai.com

Viral Paris-born favourite Junk Smash Burgers is elevating its cult smash with a festive, Dubai-exclusive twist. Available from December 20 to January 5, the limited-edition Caviar Smash Burger reimagines street food indulgence through a luxe lens. The decadent creation layers two smashed patties with melted cheese, a crispy hash brown, and a silky crème fraîche and lemon sauce, finished with a generous spoon of premium caviar sourced from Mediterranean seafood specialists Cut Caviar. Priced at Dhs99, the festive drop captures Junk’s signature simplicity while leaning into Dubai’s love for grand, statement-making bites—proof that even comfort food can dress up for the holidays.

Move into 2026 at Bohemia Beach Club, where New Year’s Eve unfolds as a high-energy, open-air beachfront celebration set against the glittering Dubai Marina skyline. Soundtracked by international DJs, the night builds to a euphoric midnight moment with front-row views of fireworks lighting up the water, immersive lighting, and FIVE Palm Jumeirah’s signature party atmosphere. Early bird tickets start from Dhs350 including drinks, making it a go-to for dance music lovers and those seeking an electric outdoor countdown by the sea.

For more information visit bohemiadubai.com

This New Year’s Eve, Sirene Beach by GAIA sets the scene for a luminous coastal celebration with its Golden Midnight Affair at J1 Beach. As the final sunset of 2025 fades, guests are invited into an evening shaped by Mediterranean warmth, elegant dining, and a seamless musical journey.

The experience centres around Sirene’s à la carte menu and a nine-course New Year’s Eve set menu (Dhs1,200 per person), featuring Aegean-inspired highlights such as tempura oysters, grilled lobster with clams and bottarga, white truffle pasta, and honeyed frozen yogurt. Live music sets the tone before DJ Ooshmann takes over from 11pm, followed by headliner DJ Deron ushering in 2026 with elevated energy.

For more information visit sirenebeach.com

Atlantis, The Palm raises the bar this New Year’s Eve with its most spectacular gala yet, headlined by global superstars Maroon 5. Set under the stars in a dazzling open-air venue, the night unfolds with welcome canapés, roaming entertainment, and a refined dining experience before the band takes to the stage for a high-energy live performance.

New for this year are the enhanced Sky Suites, offering elevated views, private butler service, and ultra-premium drinks, while younger guests are entertained in a dedicated kids’ party zone at the Asateer Tent. A truly all-encompassing celebration designed for every generation.

For more information visit atlantis.com

For a New Year’s Eve defined by glamour and movement, Lady Nara transforms into a floating stage for a midnight masquerade along the Creek. Framed by panoramic skyline views, guests are welcomed at Dhs2,000 per person, into a beautifully styled dining space where Mediterranean elegance meets subtle regional detailing. As midnight approaches, the upper deck becomes the focal point, offering open-air views of fireworks reflected across the water, a cinematic way to welcome the year ahead.

For more information visit nara.ae
