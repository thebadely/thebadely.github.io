Scotland's matches away to Greece and at home to Denmark could determine if the nation reaches a World Cup for the first time since 1998, and you watch them exclusively on the BBC.

As well as the option to watch live, you can listen to radio commentary and follow the game on the BBC Sport app and website.

Here is all you need to know so you do not miss any of the action.

You can watch Greece v Scotland on BBC Scotland, BBC Two and iPlayer from 19:15 GMT on Saturday night as Steve Clarke's side aim to set up a winner-takes-all match against Denmark at Hampden for top spot in Group C.

Steven Thompson will be joined by Neil McCann and Leanne Crichton, with James McFadden alongside Liam McLeod for commentary.

Scotland v Denmark, is also on BBC Scotland, BBC Two and iPlayer from 19:15 on Tuesday, 18 November.
